# In-Depth Person Profile Analysis: Simon Wen (Xingjian Wen)

## Executive Summary

Simon Wen, whose Chinese name is Xingjian Wen (温行健), is a 21-year-old (born circa 2005) cryptocurrency entrepreneur, decentralized finance (DeFi) farmer, and former New York University (NYU) student. Originally from Guangzhou, China, Wen has established a significant online presence under the aliases **Semona0x** and **simonwen.eth**. He is currently the founder of **VOMEUS**, a blockchain-integrated e-cigarette (VaPIN) project built on the Movement network. Demonstrating a high risk tolerance and deep immersion in crypto culture, Wen dropped out of NYU after his first semester to pursue his startup full-time with backing from Movement Labs.

## Early Life and Academic Background

### Pre-University Education in Guangzhou
Simon Wen grew up in Guangzhou, China, where he attended the prestigious **Guangzhou Foreign Language School** (广州外国语学校) for his high school education [1]. During his time there, he demonstrated exceptional academic and extracurricular capabilities, particularly in mathematics and finance.

His high school achievements include:
*   **Mathematics Competitions:** He scored in the Top 1% globally on the AMC10 (American Mathematics Competitions) with a score of 139.5/150, and placed in the Global Top 0.5% on the AIME (American Invitational Mathematics Examination) with a score of 13/15 [1] [2].
*   **Academic Research:** In 2021, he won the National First Prize at the highly competitive S.T. Yau High School Science Award (丘成桐中学科学奖) in the Economic and Financial Modeling category. His award-winning paper focused on the valuation levels of companies listed on the STAR Market based on the price-to-sales ratio [3].
*   **Extracurricular Leadership:** He served as the President of the Finance Club, Founder and President of the Cycling Club, and Consultant for the Math Club. He also co-founded the GFC Business Imitation Contest [1].

### Early Professional Experience
Even before entering university, Wen was actively involved in the financial sector. He completed an internship as a Business Analyst in the Investment Banking Department of **GF Securities Co., Ltd** (广发证券) [1]. Furthermore, he began participating in the stock and cryptocurrency markets early on, claiming to have generated over 400,000 RMB in profit from an initial capital of just 10,000 RMB [1].

### New York University (NYU)
Wen was admitted to New York University (NYU), intending to study Finance at the Stern School of Business. He enrolled as a freshman in the Fall of 2024 [4]. During his brief time at NYU, he was an active member of the **NYU Blockchain Club**, where he actively evangelized the TRON ecosystem to students across various American college blockchain clubs after meeting TRON representatives at the Consensus conference [5].

However, in December 2024, after completing only his first semester, Wen made the decision to drop out of NYU. He publicly announced this on Twitter, stating that the Movement Labs team was backing his "NYU DROPOUT journey" [6].

## Professional Ventures and Crypto Activity

### VOMEUS: The Blockchain E-Cigarette
Simon Wen is the founder and key driving force behind **VOMEUS**, a project that merges physical vaping devices with blockchain technology (a concept they refer to as "VaPIN" or DePIN for vapes) [7]. 

| VOMEUS Project Details | Information |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Network** | Movement Network (Layer 1 Blockchain) |
| **Concept** | "The first modular vape stack. Built for cult(ure)." Bridging digital identities with physical devices. |
| **Partnerships** | Partnered with Rena Labs for privacy-first health data monetization [8]. |
| **Community** | Claims over 21,000 active Discord members [9]. |
| **NFT Collection** | Launched the "Vomeus Genesis" (ERC721) collection on the Base chain via an "Early Vomers Pass" mint [10]. |

The project aims to incentivize users through token rewards while integrating deeply with crypto "degen" culture. Wen frequently promotes the project on his Twitter account (@semona0x), referring to himself as an "on-chain smoker" [11].

### Crypto Culture and Affiliations
Wen is deeply embedded in specific subcultures within the cryptocurrency space:
*   **Milady Cult / Remilia:** His Twitter bio and activity show strong affiliation with the Milady Maker NFT community and the Remilia ecosystem. He frequently references the `$CULT` token and interacts with prominent figures in that space, such as Charlotte Fang [11].
*   **DeFi Farming:** On his LinkedIn profile, he describes himself as a "Delta-neutral Defi Farmer" and a "Crypto Degen since 2019" [12].
*   **Movement Ecosystem:** Following his departure from NYU, he has aligned himself closely with the Movement Network, building VOMEUS on their infrastructure and participating in their ecosystem events [6].

### Lifestyle and Interests
In late 2022, while looking for housing in New York City, Wen posted in several Facebook groups seeking a room in the Bushwick or Bed-Stuy neighborhoods of Brooklyn. In these posts, he described himself as a 21-year-old vegan who is "spiritual-minded and emotionally open" [14]. His high school profiles also note his interest in machine learning ("炼丹"), hip-hop music, and amateur road cycling [1].

## Conclusion

Simon (Xingjian) Wen represents a modern archetype of the Gen Z crypto entrepreneur. Leveraging a strong quantitative background from his education in China, he transitioned rapidly into the high-risk, high-reward world of decentralized finance and Web3 startups. His decision to drop out of a prestigious institution like NYU to build a niche hardware-crypto project (VOMEUS) underscores his commitment to the blockchain industry and his deep integration into its unique cultural movements.
